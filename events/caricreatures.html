Rules
• Candidates can register either on day 1 of the fest or online.
• No registrations will be allowed past the deadline.
• The deadline for the registrations will be announced at a later stage.
• The event will be conducted in multiple rounds.
• There will be 4 on-fest rounds spanning over the 3 days of the festival.
• Candidates will be eliminated at the end of each round, based upon the points they scored in the previous rounds.
• The on-fest rounds will try and evaluate the participant's personality, talent, appeal, wit, humor and presence of mind during various rounds.

• The various rounds:
o Day1- Written Rounds/Personal Interview/Group Discussion.
o Day2- Series of tasks.
o Day3- Final auditorium round.

• The validity of all the tasks performed would be witnessed by an organizer at all times.
• In case of any discrepancies, the final decision will rest in the hands of the organizing committee.
• The most enigmatic duo would become the next face of Waves.

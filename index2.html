About
"Where there is light, There is shadow."


Demons cannot prosper without the goodness of angels and there is no Yin without Yang. After surfing the tides of time last year, Waves 2017 brings you a chance to explore the dark side. Join us from the 27th of October as we lose ourselves to the feeling of devilish and godly euphoria, painting ourselves with a mixture of exultation and excitement, unlike the kind you have ever seen in your life.
This year, Waves goes bigger, better and brighter as we aim to take you on a 3 day roller coaster of vivacious elation and pleasure. This Waves, be prepared to let go of your true self as you morph into an upgraded version, full of delight and uncontrolled jubilation. This Waves, be the one to write your tale afresh, a tale never to be forgotten.

Events
Beau Vista

"Dressing well is just another form of good manners"

Waves 2017's first mini-fest of the year, Beau Vista, is a combination of the best of both worlds. The art of designing, when combined with the talent of FashP gives you something unlike you have ever seen before. With 4 events under this venture, prepare the artist within you to be tested and tried as you go one on one against the best of the country.
⁠⁠⁠Florence

"The objective of art is nothing but to give life a shape"

The second stepping stone in this year's Waves, is our performing arts mini-fest, Florence, whose name aptly describes the kind of events it comprises of. With about 10 events from varying fields of Dance, Drama, and Music, prepare yourself as you take the stage and grasp the audience' awe with your amazing moves, your melodious notes or your convincing expressions as an actor.
Carpe Dictum

“The purpose of literature is to turn blood into ink”

With words as your firearm and your logic as your ammo, step into the battlefield that is Carpe Dictum and brace yourselves for 4 of the most creative and exciting events, this year. Let the writer within you grab a sheet of paper and submit yourself to the beauty of words and the depth of rhymes that surround you. After all, who better to write a tale in two shades, than a writer?
Specials

After the events that make up most of the fest, Waves’17 brings you the specials of our fest. With about 8 events that have their roots in a ton of varying fields, from Fashion to Law, from Literature to your DJing skills, be prepared to shock the crowd with the set of special talents you possess. Tickle the audience’ bellies with Show me the funny and impress strangers in an event that is the exact face of a tale in two shades: Strangely Familiar.
